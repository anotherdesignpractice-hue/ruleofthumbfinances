Rule of Thumb Finances

I made this tool after noticing that people, whether they made a modest or high salary, started with the same rule of thumbs. I am not a finance professional, I simply put together figures I noticed a lot of people use. You're free to use this as a starting point - but note that this is not personalized financial advice and I am not liable for how you make financial decisions :) got it? Good!
